Co-creating circular economy-based models in the field of Waste Management

Co-create Circular Lab is an R&D initiative of Green Worms. The idea behind the Co-create Circular Lab is to bring together citizens, scientists, innovators, brand owners & various other stakeholders and engage with them to work on an innovative circular economy-based model in the field of Waste Management.

  • Co-creation & Open Innovation.
  • Collaboration & knowledge exchange.
  • Conduction of Pilot projects for testing & validating new ideas.
  • Mentorship for Innovations to evolve into Social-Impact & Commercial models.
